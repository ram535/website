Part02 entry point

index.html is the entry point of the whole program. We are going to add a textarea tag with these propierties id=“editor”, 80 columns width and autofocus.

 1<!DOCTYPE html>
 2<html>
 3<head>
 4    <meta charset="utf-8">
 5    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
 6    <title>part02</title>
 7    <link rel="stylesheet" href="styles.css">
 8    <link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico">
 9    <script defer src="main.dart" type="application/dart"></script>
10    <script defer src="packages/browser/dart.js"></script>
11</head>
12
13<body>
14  <div id="output"></div>
15  <textarea  id="editor"  cols="80"  autofocus></textarea>
16</body>
17</html>

That’s it. From here on the next parts of the tutorial we are just going to add functionalities based on what the user type on the <textarea></textarea> tag.

Run pub serve on the root of the project and go to http://localhost:8080/ in your browser.
